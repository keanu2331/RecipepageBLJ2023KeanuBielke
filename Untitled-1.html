About us

Welcome to Crumber art - your source for delicious cookies and sweets!

Our history

Crumber art was founded in 2024 by Ryley Cole. From the beginning, our mission was clear: to create high-quality, handmade cookies and sweets that capture the hearts of our customers. What started as a small bakery has grown into a thriving business today.

Our team

Our talented team of bakers and candy artists work passionately to create the best cookies and candies. Each member of our team brings unique skills and creativity to ensure that every Crumber art product is a true delight.

Our philosophy

At Crumber art, we believe in using the best ingredients to create products of the highest quality. We rely on traditional baking techniques and combine them with innovative ideas to create cookies and sweets that offer unforgettable taste experiences.

Our products

Our range includes a wide variety of cookies and sweets for every taste and occasion. From classic chocolate cookies to special gluten-free options, we have something for everyone. Our products are perfect as gifts or to treat yourself.

Sustainability

At Crumber art, we are aware of our environmental impact and strive to act sustainably. We use environmentally friendly packaging and support local suppliers to minimize our environmental impact.
